# ServiceNow Priority Matrix

## Priority Calculation

Priority is derived from Impact × Urgency:

| | Urgency: High | Urgency: Medium | Urgency: Low |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Impact: High** | 1 - Critical | 2 - High | 3 - Moderate |
| **Impact: Medium** | 2 - High | 3 - Moderate | 4 - Low |
| **Impact: Low** | 3 - Moderate | 4 - Low | 5 - Planning |

## SLA Targets by Priority

| Priority | Response Time | Resolution Time |
|----------|--------------|-----------------|
| 1 - Critical | 15 minutes | 4 hours |
| 2 - High | 30 minutes | 8 hours |
| 3 - Moderate | 4 hours | 24 hours |
| 4 - Low | 8 hours | 72 hours |
| 5 - Planning | 24 hours | 1 week |
